About Fabrik

Fabrik is an experimental blockchain system designed to explore the fundamental architecture behind programmable blockchains.

The project includes foundational components such as wallet generation, genesis block creation, persistent chain storage, account modeling, and a prototype virtual machine (FVM) for executing compiled bytecode.

Fabrik is built primarily as a research and experimentation platform for understanding blockchain execution environments.
